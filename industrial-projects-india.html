Safety

Safety is paramount for us. Our highly trained professionals conduct safety induction and drills. Reliability and customer satisfaction are why we consistently get repeat orders from our partners.

As an employer and a company, we feel that it is our duty to ensure that our employees work for us by placing trust for their safety in our hands. This is why we are committed to ensuring the well-being of our workers in the field to the highest of safety norms that are followed in international standards.

First-aid equipment is always available on the site and a standby vehicle is kept in place in case of any emergency. We conduct safety induction programs for new employees and have regular drills in place so that we are at our best prepared in case of any mishaps.

Furthermore, we address the risks around us and encourage our workers to regularly access their working environments and identify the risks, as and when they arise.

Our standard safety proceedure includes:

  • Safety Action Plan
  • Safety Induction
  • Tool Box Talk
  • Injury Report for First AID Cases
  • Accident Report
  • Accident Investigation Report
  • Monthly Accident Statistics
