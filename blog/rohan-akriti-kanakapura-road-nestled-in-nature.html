Rohan Akriti, Kanakapura Road – Nestled In Nature

14th February 2017

Bangalore is home to more than 10,000 millionaires. What ties them together is the fact that most of them are associated with the IT-industry. The city also sees investment not just from IT-professionals but also from High Net Worth individuals, NRIs and expatriates, who all find Bangalore peaceful, modern and pleasant. The demand for gated complexes with premium designs, multi-fold amenities, and quality construction is always buoyant. This trend has been steady, particularly in areas close to important IT-centres and secondary business districts. Rohan Builders is expanding its Bangalore footprint with a recent addition to the city’s skyline, Rohan Akriti apartments. These residential apartments are nestled in one of the most pristine locations along Kanakapura Road.

We have built a reputation for good building standards and ethical business practices. The group’s projects are characterized by rich designs, good quality fittings, innovative use of space, and well thought-out locations, since its inception in 1993.

The company upholds the concept of PLUS homes which stands for Perfect Ventilation, Lively Light, Utmost Privacy and Smart Space. We have even initiated a unique practice of rating our projects on these parameters which are there for anyone to see on our portal.

Rohan Akriti offers premium luxurious homes that tick all the boxes of a PLUS home.  Keeping in line with the company’s record, the location has been chosen with attention to detail.

Rohan Akriti- A Step Into Nature From Kanakapura Road

For Rohan Akriti we have managed to bag a location that’s just about far enough from traffic - off Kanakapura Road - and yet close enough to Kanakapura Road’s sprawling social infrastructure.

Kanakapura Road’s first attraction is that it’s in the Turahalli Forest. Rohan Akriti has lush green spaces all around elevating the idea of a home to that of a dream home. But that isn’t the only feature of the area that attracted us from all other locations in South Bangalore.

Kanakapura has a stellar reputation for its connectivity and infrastructure. First in line, when it comes to connectivity, is a 6.2 km long major metro line of the Namma Metro Phase II project in the area which will connect Puttenhalli to Anjanapura Township in less than two years making travel from Konakunte to Majestic, easy.  The planned Gottigere-Nagwara metro project on Bannerghatta Road is another option for residents on Kanakapura Road. Besides this, is the NICE expressway which offers easy access to several parts of the city, particularly North of Bangalore.

Bus stops line Kanakapura Road with regular BMTC buses going towards JP Nagar, Bannerghatta Road, Banashankari, Jayanagar, Shantinagar, Majestic, Mysore Road, IT hub- Electronic City and more. Airport buses also frequent the area via Banashankari TTMC and Bannerghatta Main Road Bus Stops.

To make things better a 35 km four-lane expressway has been sanctioned by the Bangalore Development Authority to widen the main road and connect Kanakapura, Tumkuru Road, Hosur Road, Sarjapur Road, Old Madras Road and Magadi Road.

The airport is an hour and 50 minutes away, while the nearest railway station is an hour away. 


Rohan Akriti Social Infrastructure - A Walk Away

Stores like Metro Cash and Carry, Royal Mart and Shop Well are in driving distance while popular food chains like Pizza Hut, Dominos along with South Indian chains like Adigas and Ananda Bhavan are also nearby. Besides this, there’s the BDA complex in Banashankari which offers a lot of options to its customers, and if heading out isn’t appealing to a resident then apps like Swiggy, FoodPanda and FreshMenu deliver in the area. 

Healthcare is a sneeze away as well, with multi-specialty hospitals like Fortis, Jayadeva Hospital, and Sagar Hospital along with specialty clinics offering physiotherapy, orthopedic check-ups, homeopathy, ayurveda, dentistry and more.

Kanakapura road has several popular education institutes such as - Valley School, Kumarans School, Delhi Public School, RMS International School. Higher institutions such as CMS, PESIT, Christ University, Dayanand Sagar, JSS and RV College are good options.

For entertainment, there are malls like Gopalan Innovation Mall, Meenakshi Mall and Gopalan Mall (Mysore Road). If one is looking for cultural events, then Shankara Foundation is a good place to head to and if an adventurous streak is what one is after then Kanakapura Road has regular nature walks, treks, and night camping activities. ISKCON is also set to launch an Indian version of Disneyland on a 28 acre hillock.


Rohan Akriti - The Layout

Akriti is spread across 5 acres of green space and is home to 440 homes. The project offers 6 towers, each with 2 basements and G+13 floors. The design offers a centralized social space and ensures adequate use of green space.

For starters every apartment block has a 1:1 ratio of height to distance between each corresponding block in front of it, so it does not ruin any home’s views, i.e., the shadow of one home doesn’t fall on another home. The unique façade breaks all chances of monotony with the niche, the top floor elevation projection, and the double-height terraces. It also provides for ample ventilation and sunlight to reach almost every home.

Secondly, Akriti is accessible from all four ends – from Outer Ring Road, Vasanthpura main road, NICE ring road and Bangalore-Mysore Expressway. This ensures that there’s not much travel to reach home.

The third interesting aspect of the project is how in the plans we have used the natural contour to further contribute to the PLUS homes concept. All driveways are attuned to the natural contours of the project so as to let in ample amount of sunlight.

The fourth interesting aspect is the double-height terraces which create a Venturi effect and offer excellent wind-flow.

Further large wardrobe recesses keep bedroom areas clear and open to personalization.

Aesthetics of a home reflect the personality of the family living in the home and an important part of achieving good aesthetics is the design. Akriti homes ensure good use of space, such that there isn’t a single corner of your home that you think would be a better addition to another room.

Akriti offers a range of homes, namely 1, 1.5, 2, 2.5 and 3 BHK premium apartments. Possession of Akriti’s Phase-1 apartments is expected to commence by December 2019.


Rohan Akriti- Amenities On Offer

True to the central idea of the project being in the lap of nature and offering a sense of peace and calm, amenities at Rohan Akriti are essentially bound to nature. Akriti’s building blocks have a linear layout, and that’s enabled the amenities to spread across three green spaces, namely the Relax, Refresh and Rejoice Gardens.

The Relax Garden has a yoga room, meditation gardens, and open lawns – serving as a place to retreat to after a stressful day or a weekend rejuvenation space. 

The Refresh Garden offers a swimming pool along with the 17,000 sq. ft. clubhouse- all inclusive of a gym, multipurpose hall, guest rooms, crèche, badminton court, squash court. An apt name for the energy-brimming and active space, the Refresh Garden is an everyday affair for exercise and recreation.

The Rejoice Garden houses the children’s play area, chess, the main garden and the tennis court. A place that has children and high energy sports is bound to be bustling with noise and laughter and that’s what this space encapsulates with its name.

The project also offers a party hall, cricket practice pitch, a health centre and, needless to say, landscaped gardens.

Rohan Akriti - Are You An Ideal Resident?

Akriti offers premium homes at a competitive rate and considering the location, the connectivity and proximity to South Bangalore and North Bangalore, Akriti is ideal for someone who is working and on a relatively smaller budget.

Akriti is also ideal for someone who wishes to have a home in an area that’s at the peak of its development at good rates. With a good social infrastructure in place already, Kanakapura Road sees only further development and perks being added to it.

If either of the above two options don’t quite resonate with you, then the third reason why Akriti could be what you’re looking for is if you’re looking for a smart investment option. Akriti offers excellent amenities at rates that are unmatched in Kanakapura Road.

If one has an office in Bannerghatta, Silk Board, Electronic City, Banashankari, Kumarswamy Layout or any other South Bangalore location, Kanakapura is a no-brainer and Rohan Akriti is good to be considered for various factors listed above.
