When you love your work, it shows. So, it is no surprise that Rohan Group today has built itself a name across the Real Estate, Infrastructure & Industrial contracting sectors. Rohan Builders started in 1993, and now has a team of over 1,000 professionals and landmarks sprawling over 15 million square feet across the country. It is consistently rated by CRISIL as DA2+ for last 10 years, signifying Rohan’s ability in executing projects as per the specified quality, adhering to timelines.

Rohan Group has been the recipient of many esteemed recognitions. However, the trust showed by existing customers who have referred over 32% of all homes sold is the most cherished by all at the Rohan family. Making a selection of the things that matter most to great living, the group conceptualized PLUS Homes - Perfect Ventilation, Lively Light, Utmost Privacy, Smart Space, an integral aspect of all Rohan homes. Rohan has also introduced the Tunnel form technology, a rapid and advanced construction technique, that helps build one floor a day with much better quality and durability than conventional construction.

Rohan Group’s excellence in design & innovation is also weaved into the nation’s infrastructure – the Amritsar Wagah NH1 that connects India to Pakistan, and other roads, tunnels, bridges across Punjab, Rajasthan, Maharashtra, to cite a few. It has also led large multinational clients such as Coke, Pepsi, HUL, ITC, Bajaj, Tata, Mahindra, Cairn to trust Rohan Group with repeat orders.

Rohan Group encourages both innovation and accountability by giving freedom within boundaries, making it one of the finest places to work. With feet firmly on ground and vision far ahead in the future, Rohan Group continues to inspire and lead the industry from the front.

Our Story

2012

Our First Step

Inception of Rohan Builders (India) Pvt Ltd, the industrial contracting division, and Rohan Builders & Developers Pvt. Ltd, the real estate development division of Rohan Group

Our first project successfully completed - Modi-Hoover vacuum cleaner manufacturing plant.

1995

Our Foray into Real Estate

Completion of 1st residential project - in Tejas Society, Kothrud

1996

Our First Award

Award for Excellence in Construction with Quality, Speed, and Durability from the Builders’ Association of India for the Ruby Hall Clinic, Pune

1998

Opening Happiness

The first plant of Coca-Cola was completed. Rohan Group went on to build 18 more plants for Coca-Cola in India.

1999

Beginning of a New Journey

Rohan Group partners with Rajdeep Group to set up the infrastructure development company, which today manages road assets across Punjab, Maharashtra, Rajasthan, and Kerala.

2000

Start Of a Revolution

The launch of Rohan Garima built on Rohan’s trademark PLUS Home concept. Rohan brings in a host of firsts for the real estate industry like compensation for delay clause in customer agreement, and 5-year maintenance contract.

2001

Awards Galore

Rohan builders receive Best Project award for Innovative and Unique Creation from the AESA Beharay Rathi Award for the Project - Rohan Retreat at Chakan, Maharashtra.

2002

Birth of an Icon

The launch of the iconic 1 Modibaug, till today one of the highest appreciated project in Pune, and also which commands the highest rate even today across Pune.

2004

Exploring new Horizons

Rohan Builders expand its presence in Bangalore with the launch of its Rohan Vasantha project, marketed with the iconic ‘No Common Walls’ campaign. It was one of the fastest selling projects in Bangalore

2006

Reaching the Stars

Rohan builders were awarded the CRISIL DA2+ rating for real estate developers, the highest rating given to any developer in India. Rohan Group was the only one of the two companies in the country to have maintained this rating in the economic downturn of 2008.

2007

On The Red Carpet

Rohan Madhuban’s Rusty campaign nominated for Media Lions at Cannes, the first and still the only real estate company in India to have its ad campaigns nominated for this prestigious award.

Jury Recommendation Award for “Best Construction Management” for Tata BlueScope Steel Ltd.

2008

The Big Day

Rohan Mithila was launched. This was one of the biggest projects in Pune at that time and outperformed in an otherwise sluggish market.

2009

To New Alliances

Rohan enters into a partnership with IndoSpace to develop one of India’s first modern logistics parks

2010

The Crown Jewel

Rohan builders won the ‘Best Luxury - Residential Project’ for Rohan Madhuban and ‘Best Residential Project’ for Rohan Jharoka in 2010.

2011

Safety First

Rohan builders were awarded the prestigious PCERF (Pune Construction Engineering Research Foundation) First Safety Award (Silver Cap) for Rohan Mithila

2012

10 Million Smiles Delivered

Rohan Builders successfully completes 10 million sq ft of construction across its various businesses

2013

Another feather in the Cap

More glory for Mithila project; TESE (Technology, Ecology & Environmental, Social & Economic) Award to Rohan Mithila

2015

Going Turbo with Tunnel technology

Rohan Builders launch Rohan Abhilasha and with it the Tunnel Form Technology, a rapid and advanced construction technique that builds one floor a day.
